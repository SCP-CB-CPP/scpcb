Place .ogg/.wav files in this folder to play them through the radio or as custom music while in game.

Radio:
When you find a radio, go to channel 1 to listen to your tracks.
To have the game re-check this directory for new files, go into the options menu and click the "Check user tracks" button.

Custom Music:
Simply go into the console and type "playmusic $filename.ogg/.wav".
